Safe Link Checker

Enable on Google Search
How to Use
  1. Perform a search on Google.
  2. Click the 'Safety Check' button next to any search result.
  3. The result will appear as a badge: Safe, Caution, or Risky.
  4. For 'Caution' or 'Risky' results, hover over the 'Reason' button to see why.
Result Guide
Safe Generally safe to visit.
Caution Be careful. May use URL shorteners or have other minor issues.
Risky High risk. Avoid visiting. (e.g., no HTTPS, phishing keywords)
